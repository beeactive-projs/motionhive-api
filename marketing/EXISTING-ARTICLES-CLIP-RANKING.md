# Existing Blog Articles - Clip Potential Ranking

> Ranking of the 8 current blog articles from BEST to WORST for social media clip creation.

## Rank 1: HIIT vs Strength vs Cardio: Which Training Style Fits You?
**Category**: Science | **Clip Potential**: 12-15 pieces

**Why #1**: Comparison content is the #1 performing format on short-form video. Built-in tension ("which one?"), three-way comparison maps perfectly to visual formats, and fitness audiences love settling this debate. Drives comments AND saves.

| Clip Idea | Format | Platform | Hook |
|-----------|--------|----------|------|
| "HIIT burns more fat in 20 min than cardio in 60" myth bust | 30s Reel | IG, TikTok | "Your cardio session is lying to you." |
| 24hr calorie burn comparison (afterburn) | Motion graphic | TikTok, Shorts | "Nobody tells you what happens 24 hours AFTER your workout." |
| "I tried all 3 for 30 days" mini doc | Day-in-life | TikTok, Shorts | "30 days. 3 training styles. 1 winner." |
| Training personality quiz | Carousel | Instagram | "Swipe to find your training type." |
| HIIT or Strength? poll series | Story polls | IG Stories | "Stop guessing. Answer 3 questions." |
| Afterburn effect (EPOC) explainer | Whiteboard | TikTok, LinkedIn | "HIIT trainers keep winning while they sleep." |
