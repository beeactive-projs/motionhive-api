## Cross-Platform Consistency Notes

### Brand Voice
- Human, not corporate
- Bold about being free ("free forever" is a genuine differentiator)
- Community-first framing ("your community" not "our app")
- Building in public = trustworthy and unique
- Avoid fitness cliches ("transform your body", "crush your goals")

### Username Priority
1. `@beeactive` on all platforms (lock this in first)
2. `@beeactiveapp` as universal fallback
3. `@joinbeeactive` as secondary fallback

### Link Strategy by Stage
- **Pre-launch (now)**: Single landing page link everywhere
- **Post-launch**: Still prefer direct link over Linktree; only use aggregator if 3+ meaningful destinations

### Profile Photos
- Use the BeeActive logo (lightning bolt icon) as profile photo across all platforms
- Use a branded banner/cover image showing the tagline "Where active communities come together"
