# BeeActive - Video Clip Templates & Article-to-Clip Pipeline

> 6 reusable video formats + systematic process for turning any blog article into 10+ content pieces.

---

## Template 1: Myth Buster

**Structure (25-30 seconds):**
| Timestamp | What Happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| 0-3 sec | Hook: State the myth as if it's true. "Everyone says [myth]." |
| 3-8 sec | Build: "And honestly? It makes sense. But..." |
| 8-20 sec | Bust: "Here's what actually happens..." + the real data/insight |
| 20-25 sec | Flip: "So the next time someone tells you [myth], show them this." |
| 25-30 sec | CTA: "Follow for more fitness facts that actually matter." |

**Best Platform**: TikTok (highest engagement), Instagram Reels

**Hook Examples:**
- "Cardio burns the most fat. Right? Wrong."
- "You need to work out every day to see results. Let me stop you right there."
- "Online training doesn't work as well as in-person. Actually..."
- "Meal prep means eating the same thing every day. No it doesn't."
- "Motivation is what keeps you going. That's the biggest lie in fitness."

**Visual**: Talking head. Bold text overlay for the myth in red, truth in green. Quick cut between myth and bust.

**Caption Template:**
```
[Myth] is what everyone believes. But science says otherwise.

Save this before your next workout.

#FitnessMyths #FitnessTips #[TopicHashtag] #BeeActive #FYP
```

## Template 2: Hot Take

**Structure (20-25 seconds):**
| Timestamp | What Happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| 0-3 sec | Hook: "Unpopular opinion:" + the take. No buildup. |
| 3-15 sec | Defense: Why you believe this. One strong argument. |
| 15-20 sec | Challenge: "Fight me in the comments." or "Agree or disagree?" |
| 20-25 sec | Brand tie: Subtle connection to BeeActive if relevant. |

**Best Platform**: TikTok (drives comments), LinkedIn (text version)

**Hook Examples:**
- "Unpopular opinion: personal trainers should stop selling 1-on-1 sessions."
- "Hot take: the gym is the worst place to start a fitness journey."
- "Controversial: motivation is a scam. Here's what actually works."
- "Nobody wants to hear this: your workout routine isn't the problem. Your community is."
- "The fitness industry profits from you being alone. Think about that."

**Visual**: Close-up talking head. Slightly confrontational energy. Text overlay of the hot take. No B-roll needed.

**Caption Template:**
```
I said what I said.

[1-2 sentence expansion of the take]

Agree or disagree? Drop your take below.

#UnpopularOpinion #FitnessTake #[TopicHashtag] #BeeActive
```

## Template 3: Did You Know

**Structure (20-25 seconds):**
| Timestamp | What Happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| 0-3 sec | Hook: "Did you know..." + surprising stat/fact |
| 3-10 sec | Context: Quick explanation of why this matters |
| 10-18 sec | Implication: "Which means..." + practical takeaway |
| 18-25 sec | CTA: "Save this. Follow for more." |

**Best Platform**: Instagram Reels (high save rate), TikTok

**Hook Examples:**
- "Did you know you work 200% harder when someone is watching you exercise?"
- "Did you know the 10,000 steps goal was invented for a marketing campaign?"
- "Did you know 80% of people who start a fitness program quit within 6 weeks?"
- "Did you know meal prepping saves the average person $150/month?"
- "Did you know walking 20 minutes a day equals running 50 marathons in a year?"

**Visual**: Text-heavy overlay with the stat front and center. Talking head or B-roll fitness footage. Use a "zoom in" effect on the key number.

**Caption Template:**
```
This stat changed how I think about fitness.

[Expand on the implication in 1-2 sentences]

Save this and share with someone who needs to hear it.

#FitnessFacts #DidYouKnow #[TopicHashtag] #BeeActive
```

## Template 4: This or That

**Structure (15-20 seconds):**
| Timestamp | What Happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| 0-3 sec | Hook: "Online or in-person? Answer NOW." Direct, urgent. |
| 3-8 sec | Your take: "I'll go first. [Your answer] because [1 reason]." |
| 8-15 sec | The nuance: "But honestly, it depends on [this factor]." |
| 15-20 sec | CTA: "Drop yours in the comments. No wrong answers." |

**Best Platform**: TikTok (comment engine), Instagram Stories (poll sticker)

**Hook Examples:**
- "HIIT or strength training? Choose now."
- "Online trainer or in-person trainer? Go."
- "Solo workouts or group workouts? Don't think, just answer."
- "Morning workout or evening workout? There's a right answer."
- "Gym membership or home setup? Be honest."

**Visual**: Split screen or rapid cut between the two options. Use text that fills the screen. Point left/right. High energy.

**Caption Template:**
```
[Option A] or [Option B]? No fence-sitting.

Drop your answer below. I'm reading every comment.

#ThisOrThat #FitnessDebate #[TopicHashtag] #BeeActive #FYP
```

## Template 5: 3 Things

**Structure (25-30 seconds):**
| Timestamp | What Happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| 0-3 sec | Hook: "3 things [audience] needs to hear right now." |
| 3-10 sec | Thing 1: Short, punchy. One sentence + brief expansion. |
| 10-18 sec | Thing 2: Same format. |
| 18-25 sec | Thing 3: The strongest one. Save the best for last. |
| 25-30 sec | CTA: "Which one hit hardest? Comment the number." |

**Best Platform**: All platforms. Universal format.

**Hook Examples:**
- "3 things fitness instructors won't tell you."
- "3 things that matter more than your workout."
- "3 signs your fitness community is actually toxic."
- "3 reasons you keep quitting fitness (and it's not your fault)."
- "3 things I wish I knew before starting meal prep."

**Visual**: Talking head with text overlay numbering (1, 2, 3) appearing as each point is made. Clean cuts between each. No transitions needed.

**Caption Template:**
```
3 things [audience] needs to hear right now:

1. [Thing 1]
2. [Thing 2]
3. [Thing 3]

Which one hit different? Comment the number.

#FitnessTips #[TopicHashtag] #BeeActive
```

## Template 6: Story Time

**Structure (45-60 seconds):**
| Timestamp | What Happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| 0-3 sec | Hook: Start mid-story. "So last week I..." or "I watched this happen..." |
| 3-15 sec | Setup: Quick context. Who, where, what happened. |
| 15-35 sec | The turning point: The moment everything changed. |
| 35-50 sec | The lesson: What you learned. Make it universal. |
| 50-60 sec | CTA: "Has this ever happened to you? Tell me below." |

**Best Platform**: TikTok (watch time), Instagram Reels

**Hook Examples:**
- "I watched a fitness instructor lose her entire class in one week. Here's what happened."
- "Someone sent me a DM that made me rethink everything about fitness apps."
- "I joined a running group that changed my life. But not because of the running."
- "Last month I almost quit building BeeActive. Here's the honest reason."
- "A personal trainer told me something I'll never forget."

**Visual**: Pure talking head. Closer than usual. Genuine emotion. No graphics needed. The story IS the content. Low background music.

**Caption Template:**
```
This happened to me and I can't stop thinking about it.

[1-2 sentence teaser that doesn't give away the ending]

Has this happened to you? Drop your story below.

#StoryTime #FitnessJourney #[TopicHashtag] #BeeActive
```

# Article-to-Clip Pipeline

> Systematic process for extracting content from ANY blog article.

## Step 1: Read the Article and Extract

Pull out these elements:
- [ ] **The Hook**: What's the most counterintuitive or surprising claim?
- [ ] **The Stat**: Any numbers, percentages, or data points?
- [ ] **The List**: Any numbered lists or bullet points?
- [ ] **The Story**: Any personal anecdote or case study?
- [ ] **The Myth**: Any common belief the article challenges?
- [ ] **The Hot Take**: What opinion would spark debate?
- [ ] **The Comparison**: Any A vs B framing?
- [ ] **Quotable Lines**: 2-3 sentences that stand alone as wisdom

## Step 2: Map to Formats

| Extracted Element | Content Piece |
|-------------------|---------------|
| Hook + Stat | TikTok "Did You Know" |
| Myth + Reality | TikTok/Reel "Myth Buster" |
| Hot Take | TikTok "Hot Take" + LinkedIn text post |
| List (3+ items) | "3 Things" Reel + Instagram Carousel |
| Comparison | "This or That" TikTok + Carousel |
| Story | "Story Time" TikTok/Reel |
| Quotable Lines | 2 Quote Graphics for Stories |
| Full article summary | LinkedIn thought piece + Twitter thread |

## Step 3: Production Checklist

For each article, produce these 10 pieces:

### 3 TikTok/Reel Scripts
- [ ] **Script 1 (Myth Buster or Hot Take)** — from the article's contrarian angle
- [ ] **Script 2 (Did You Know or 3 Things)** — from the article's data/list
- [ ] **Script 3 (Story Time or This or That)** — from the article's narrative

### 1 Instagram Carousel
- [ ] **Slide 1**: Hook (bold claim from article)
- [ ] **Slides 2-6**: One insight per slide
- [ ] **Slide 7**: CTA to blog + BeeActive

### 2 Quote Graphics
- [ ] **Quote 1**: The article's most shareable one-liner
- [ ] **Quote 2**: The article's most practical takeaway

### 1 LinkedIn Post
- [ ] First-person perspective, NOT a summary
- [ ] Pick ONE idea, make it personal
- [ ] End with a genuine question

### 1 Twitter Thread
- [ ] Tweet 1: Hook as provocation + "(thread)"
- [ ] Tweets 2-7: One idea per tweet
- [ ] Tweet 8: CTA + BeeActive mention

### 1 Email Snippet
- [ ] Subject line: Article hook as question
- [ ] 3 bullet point takeaways
- [ ] Link to full article
- [ ] Reply-driving question at end

### 1 Facebook Post
- [ ] 100-150 words, conversational
- [ ] Link in COMMENTS (not post body)
- [ ] End with direct question

## Example: Extracting from "Why Accountability Is the Real Secret"

**Hook**: "Motivation is unreliable. Accountability is the system."
**Stat**: "Most people quit when nobody notices they're gone"
**Myth**: "You just need more motivation/discipline"
**Hot Take**: "Accountability isn't about someone yelling at you"
**Comparison**: Solo vs having an accountability system
**Quotable**: "The people who get results aren't more disciplined. They just have better systems."

### Resulting Content:
1. **TikTok Myth Buster**: "You need more motivation. WRONG. You need more accountability."
2. **TikTok Story Time**: "I watched someone quit fitness because nobody texted them after they missed one session."
3. **TikTok 3 Things**: "3 types of accountability that actually work in fitness"
4. **Carousel**: "Why accountability beats motivation every time (5 slides)"
5. **Quote 1**: "The people who get results aren't more disciplined. They just have better systems."
6. **Quote 2**: "Good accountability never feels like punishment. It feels like having someone in your corner."
7. **LinkedIn**: "Unpopular opinion: Accountability is not a mindset problem. It's an infrastructure problem..."
8. **Thread**: "Most fitness advice is about willpower. But the data shows something completely different. (thread)"
9. **Email**: "Is your fitness routine missing this one thing?"
10. **Facebook**: "What made you actually stick to fitness? Not what inspired you — what made you STAY?"
