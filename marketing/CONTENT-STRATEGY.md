# BeeActive Pre-Launch Content Strategy

> Complete content marketing playbook for building audience before product launch.

---

## 1. Content Pillars

Every piece of content must fit into at least one of these 5 pillars.

### Pillar 1: The Instructor Economy
For coaches, PTs, and fitness instructors. Content about building a real community, not just a client list. Scheduling, retention, the business side of fitness.

### Pillar 2: Community Over Solo
The science and practice of group accountability. Why people quit solo and stay when they have people around them. Hits both audiences: instructors want to build community; users want to be convinced group fitness works.

### Pillar 3: Fitness Without the Gatekeeping
Accessible fitness. No gym required. No expensive equipment. No shame. Local group runs, park workouts, online sessions. This is the brand's soul.

### Pillar 4: Building in Public (BeeActive Story)
Our journey building the platform. What we're building, why, what broke, what shipped. This is our moat — nobody can replicate it. Builds trust and attracts early adopters.

### Pillar 5: Habits, Consistency, and the Long Game
Not hacks. Not 30-day transformations. The boring truth about what works over years. Ages well, ranks on Google, filters in the right users.

## 2. Weekly Content Calendar Framework

Designed for a solo founder or very small team. Batch everything on one day.

| Day | Content | Platform | Pillar |
|-----|---------|----------|--------|
| **Monday** | SEO Blog Post (1,200-2,500 words) | Blog | 1 or 5 |
| **Tuesday** | Instagram Carousel (repurposed from blog) | Instagram | Same as blog |
| **Wednesday** | LinkedIn Thought Piece (150-300 words) | LinkedIn | 1 or 4 |
| **Thursday** | TikTok/Reel (60-90 sec, from blog) | TikTok + IG Reels | Same as blog |
| **Friday** | Twitter/X Thread + Email Newsletter | Twitter + Email | Same as blog |
| **Saturday** | Facebook community post + batch next week | Facebook | 2 or 3 |
| **Sunday** | Weekly reflection (building in public) | IG Stories + LinkedIn | 4 |

**Batch Day**: Saturday morning — write the blog, repurpose everything in 2-3 hours, schedule the week.

## 4. Content Repurposing System

### 1 Blog Article → 10+ Pieces

| # | Output | Time | Tool |
|---|--------|------|------|
| 1 | Instagram Carousel (5-7 slides) | 20 min | Canva template |
| 2 | TikTok Script (60-90 sec) | 15 min film + 10 min edit | CapCut |
| 3 | Instagram Reel (same as TikTok, no watermark) | 5 min | Native IG upload |
| 4 | LinkedIn Thought Piece (150-300 words) | 15 min | Direct post |
| 5 | Twitter/X Thread (6-10 tweets) | 15 min | Direct post |
| 6 | Facebook Post (100-150 words) | 10 min | Link in comments |
| 7 | Email Newsletter (200 words + link) | 15 min | Brevo |
| 8 | Pinterest Pin (1000x1500px) | 10 min | Canva |
| 9 | Quote Graphics for Stories (2-3) | 10 min | Canva |
| 10 | Reddit Comment/Post | 10 min | Manual |

**Total time per article**: ~2 hours to repurpose across all channels.

### Repurposing Templates

**Instagram Carousel:**
- Slide 1 (Hook): Bold statement from article
- Slides 2-6: One insight per slide
- Slide 7 (CTA): "Full article at beeactive.io/blog"

**TikTok Script:**
- Hook (0-3 sec): Most counterintuitive line. No intro.
- Problem (3-20 sec): Describe the viewer's situation
- Insight (20-50 sec): Main idea, fast cuts
- CTA (50-60 sec): "Link in bio for full breakdown"

**LinkedIn Post:**
- Line 1: Bold claim or personal admission
- Lines 2-5: Story leading to the idea
- Lines 6-10: Insight with one example
- Final line: Genuine question for comments

**Twitter Thread:**
- Tweet 1: Title as provocation + "(thread)"
- Tweets 2-7: One idea per tweet, standalone
- Tweet 8: Conclusion + BeeActive mention + link
- Tweet 9: "RT tweet 1 to help a fitness instructor"

## 5. Email List Building

### Waitlist as Primary Opt-In
"Get early access" converts better than "subscribe." People on a waitlist feel like insiders.

### Email Sequence (5 emails, automated)
1. **Immediate**: Welcome + what we're building + why
2. **Day 3**: Personal story — why you started
3. **Day 7**: "Are you an instructor or member?" (segments the list)
4. **Day 14**: Share one relevant blog article
5. **Day 21**: Product update with screenshot/mockup

### Lead Magnets
- **For instructors**: "Free Fitness Instructor Community Starter Kit" (30-day challenge template + session planning guide)
- **For users**: "Find Your Fitness Community" 5-minute self-assessment quiz

### Placement
- Top of every blog article: inline CTA banner
- End of every article: content upgrade (article-specific resource)
- Exit intent popup on blog pages only
- Social media bios: waitlist link

### Tool
**Brevo** (formerly Sendinblue) — free up to 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts.

## 6. User-Generated Content Strategy

### Founding Member Program (20-50 people)
Recruit from: personal network, waitlist, Reddit/Facebook fitness communities

**Give them:**
- Private Discord/Slack channel
- Direct input on features
- "Founding Member" badge at launch
- Early access to everything

**Ask for:**
- 30-second voice note about why they joined
- Photo of their workout setup
- Answer: "What's the hardest thing about staying consistent?"

Turn their answers into content: quote graphics, carousel posts, blog pull quotes.

### Pre-Launch Challenge
**"30 Days of Movement"** — post a photo/video of any daily movement. Use `#BeeActiveMovement`. Curate and reshare. No app required.

### Interview Series
**"Real Fitness Communities"** — interview running club organizers, CrossFit owners, online yoga instructors. Becomes blog articles, social clips, and partnerships. Each interviewee shares the published piece with their audience.

## 8. KPIs to Track

### Monthly Targets

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 3 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Waitlist signups | 50 | 500 |
| Email open rate | 40%+ | 45%+ |
| Email click rate | 5%+ | 8%+ |
| Instagram followers | 200 | 1,000 |
| LinkedIn followers | 100 | 500 |
| TikTok followers | 0 | 500 |

### Content Metrics
- Blog: organic sessions per article, time on page (2:30+ target), bounce rate (<70%)
- Social: comments per post (rising = good), shares, saves
- Email: replies (gold — become testimonials and article ideas)

### The One Metric That Matters
**Waitlist signups.** If a channel isn't converting to signups within 4 weeks, change the CTA, content, or deprioritize.
