## Post 8 — Week 4, Thursday | Text
**Purpose**: Lessons learned

```
10 things I've learned building BeeActive in the first 6 weeks:

1. Ship ugly. Nobody cares about perfect. They care about useful.
2. Talk to users before you build the feature, not after.
3. The competitor you're scared of is probably not stealing your users.
4. "Free" is not a business model. Community is.
5. Fitness instructors are wildly underserved by software.
6. Blog content before launch is not vanity. It's trust.
7. The first 50 followers matter more than the first 500.
8. Ask for feedback, not validation.
9. Building in public is uncomfortable and accelerates everything.
10. The product is not the thing. The community is the thing.

What would you add?
```
